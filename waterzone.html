💦 Water Zone & Swimming Policies

🏊 General Rules

  • All campers must wear a Jellystone Park™ wristband to access the Wibit. Sign up at the golf shack.
  • Maximum 10 guests per hour for safety and liability.
  • Small children must wear swim diapers in pool and Water Zone areas.
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult in the pool area.
  • Failure to comply with Water Zone rules may result in eviction from the park.

🏞️ Swimming & Fishing Pond

  • Open from 10am to dusk.
  • Children under 15 must be supervised by an adult in the swimming pond.

💦 Waterslide Regulations

  • Minimum height: 42 inches
  • Maximum weight: 250 lbs

🛶 Kayak & Tube Rentals

  • Kayak rental: $35 + tax for 8 miles (~4 hours), includes transportation.
  • Park-owned canoes/kayaks may not be taken downriver from Natural Bridge Campground.
  • Own canoe? Shuttle up-river: $50 (+ possible gas surcharge).
  • Own kayak? Shuttle up-river: $25 (+ possible gas surcharge).
  • No personal tubes permitted.
  • Tube rental (with life jacket & transport): $20 + tax per guest.
  • See the river trips & rentals page for detailed pricing.

🎈 Jumping Pillows

  • No jumping while wet – slippery surfaces can cause injuries.
  • No flipping – jumping pillows can become crowded and unsafe.
